# Global Sales Analytics Dashboard (2011 - 2016)

An interactive, data-driven Excel dashboard designed to analyze global retail sales, profitability, and customer demographics. This project transforms raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence, helping stakeholders track key performance indicators (KPIs) across multiple dimensions.

## 📊 Dashboard Overview
<img width="1869" height="753" alt="DashBoard" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/eb43434a-0a9f-48d5-813d-e6e012c76d5b" />

The dashboard provides a holistic view of business performance, featuring dynamic slicers that filter data by **Day, Month, Year, and Country**. 

### Key Insights Tracked:
* **Financial Performance:** Global Revenue ($85.2M total) and Profit ($32.2M total) trends over a 6-year period (2011–2016).
* **Product Analytics:** Cost breakdown by Category (Bikes, Accessories, Clothing) and Sub-Category (Road Bikes, Helmets, etc.).
* **Geographic Distribution:** Revenue and order quantities mapped across key markets, including the United States, Australia, United Kingdom, Germany, France, and Canada.
* **Demographics:** Sales distribution across age groups, identifying "Adults (35-64)" as the primary revenue driver.
* **Operational Efficiency:** Daily order quantity fluctuations (`Qty Of Prod Per Day`) to monitor supply chain consistency.

---

## 🛠️ Features & Functionalities

* **Dynamic Slicers:** Seamlessly filter the entire dashboard by specific dates or geographic regions.
* **Advanced Pivot Tables & Charts:** Structured data backend driving dual-axis trend lines, breakdown bar charts, and demographic columns.
* **Proportional Donut & Pie Charts:** Quick visual distribution of market share and order volumes by country.
